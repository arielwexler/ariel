Ariel is the co-creator of Burner Phone, a print only, hyperlocal zine encouraging offline creativity and connection. Each issue is themed. We host events like writing groups, launch parties, readings, and zine fairs. We are based in Bushwick, Brooklyn.

Past themes include:

  1. Manifesto
  2. Heat Stroke
  3. Cars
  4. The Mirror Stage
  5. The Commitment Issue
