About Ariel

Ariel Wexler is a Master's Student in Library and Information Sciences at Pratt Institute. She previously worked in law, operations, and the arts. She graduated from George Washington University in 2021 with a Bachelor's Degree in American Studies and a double minor in Political Science and Journalism. Ariel is currently working as the Research Assistant to Professor Sara Schmerler's Shelf Portrait Initiative. She is based in Ridgewood, Queens.
